Why ATO?

Here at the Delta Rho Chapter of Alpha Tau Omega we build men that are taught to always do what is right no matter what it takes. The friendships and bonds you create here will be beneficial to you throughout every chapter of your life. We not only focus on academics but qualities that help you serve humanity. We help you make connections that are able to assist you with any help you need, whenever you need. At ATO, you are destined to succeed.

Alpha Tau Omega Creed

To bind men together in a brotherhood based upon eternal and immutable principles, with a bond as strong as right itself and as lasting as humanity ; to know no North, no South, no East, no West, but to know man as man, to teach that true men the world over should stand together.
-Otis Allan Glazebrook, 1880
