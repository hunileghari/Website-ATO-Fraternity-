FAQs


Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the benefits of being in a fraternity?
    • You are able to make lasting connections in the professional sphere.
    • Build friendships that last a lifetime with people from all over the world.
    • Being able to balance academics as well as your social interactions.
    • Leadership from both alumni mentors and upperclassmen.
    • Leadership opportunities within the house and the surrounding community.
  • Is it mandatory to live inside the chapter house?
    • You may chose to live in any year that you wish once you complete New Member Education.
    • Some brothers chose to live out junior year, but almost all seniors live in.
  • How much does it cost to live in a fraternity house?
    • The cost of living in a fraternity house depends chapter to chapter. Usually it is either the same cost as DePauw housing or less than that.
  • Is there a GPA requirement to join a fraternity?
    • Yes, you are required to have a GPA of at least 2.6 in order to rush and be a part of a fraternity chapter.
  • Will my grades be affected by joining a fraternity?
    • Our members have proved to have great academic standards after joining the fraternity. They are able to learn skills that help them balance their academics and as well as social activities.
  • Can first years join a fraternity?
    • Yes, first years are eligible to take part in formal recruitment after completing their first semester of college and obtaining a GPA of 2.6.
  • When does fraternity recruitment take place?
    • Formal recruitment starts on the 24th of January and ends on the 26th which is right after winter term ends and right before the start of Spring semester.
