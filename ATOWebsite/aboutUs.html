About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote brotherly love between the members of our organization.
A brotherhood that is free from the issues of race, injustice and inequality.
We hope to create gentlemen who stand together and prove to be leaders in every walk of life.
We believe that morals are more important than politics and our members are taught to recognize merit wherever found.




Our House

Our chapter is the large white house located at 504 East Seminary St, across from Delta Tau Delta and sharing a parking lot with Beta, Sig Chi, and Pi Phi.
Our house can sleep 120 comfortably, but we usually maintain about 50-60 guys, so everyone has enough room to spread out.
We hold brotherhood events frequently to reinforce our bonds as brothers and it gives us an opportunity to get out of Greencastle.

Our History

Alpha Tau Omega was founded on September 11, 1865 at the Virginia Military Institute by Alfred Marshall, Otis Allen Glazebrook and Eskine Mayo Ross.

The purpose of ATO was to form a sense of brotherhood between Christians to help promote harmony between the North and South after the American Civil War.

The organization has 250 existing chapters and around 229,000 members worldwide.

Alpha Tau Omega Creed

To bind men together in a brotherhood based upon eternal and immutable principles, with a bond as strong as right itself and as lasting as humanity ; to know no North, no South, no East, no West, but to know man as man, to teach that true men the world over should stand together.
-Otis Allan Glazebrook, 1880
