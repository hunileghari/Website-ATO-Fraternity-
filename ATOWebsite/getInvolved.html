Rush ATO!

Rush at DePauw is a 3 day process. Round 1 takes place on the first day; you go to every single house, get a tour of the house and meet the brothers. Round 2 takes place on the second day; you go to your top 5 picked houses; you get to know the brothers and the house better Round 3 takes place on the third and final day; you go to your top 2 houses and get to know the brothers and the house on a deeper level and also start finalizing your top choice. You select your final choice and if they select you back, you get your bid that night.




Rush Advice

Get to know all the guys and see where you fit in.

Be a leader not a follower.

Join a house where you feel like you fit in not where your friends/family want you to go.

It is important to join a house where you can see yourself growing as a person.
